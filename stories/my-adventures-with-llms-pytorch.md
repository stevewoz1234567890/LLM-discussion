# My Adventures with Large Language Models (book + code)

**My Adventures with Large Language Models** is a **Leanpub** book that walks through **five architectures from scratch in PyTorch**, aimed at readers who use LLMs daily and want intuition for what happens inside something like `model.generate()`.

### What it covers (as described)

- **Vanilla Transformer**
- **GPT-2** (loads **OpenAI** weights)
- **Llama 3.2-3B** (loads **Meta** weights)
- **KV cache + GQA**
- **DeepSeek-style pieces:** **MLA**, **MoE**, **multi-token prediction**, **FP8**

Runnable **code** accompanies the chapters end-to-end, including **real pretrained weights** where applicable.
